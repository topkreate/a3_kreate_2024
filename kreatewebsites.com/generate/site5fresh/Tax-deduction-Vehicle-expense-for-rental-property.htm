VEHICLE EXPENSES

You can deduct any ordinary and necessary car and truck expenses that are related to the management, conservation, or maintenance of your rental property. Examples include driving - * to the property to collect rent payments * to the property for maintenance and repairs (painting, plumbing, yard work, etc.) * to the store to purchase materials for rental repairs * to the bank to take care of rental property accounts (deposits, mortgage payments, etc.) or to deposit royalty checks * to the post office to mail rent receipts or mortgage payments.
